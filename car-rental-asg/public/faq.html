Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car? +
Browse available cars on our “Cars” page, choose your preferred vehicle, and click “Book Now.” Fill in your pickup details, confirm your booking, and you’re all set!
Can I modify or cancel a booking? +
Yes, you can manage your bookings in the “My Bookings” section. Depending on the policy, cancellation or modification fees may apply.
What documents are required to rent a car? +
You’ll need a valid driver’s license, an ID (passport or national ID), and a valid payment method. International renters may need an International Driving Permit (IDP).
Is delivery and pickup service available? +
Yes, we provide flexible delivery options. You can meet the host at a pickup location or have the vehicle delivered to your preferred address for an additional fee.
Do I need to pay a deposit? +
A refundable security deposit may be required based on the car type. It will be refunded upon safe return of the vehicle.
What happens if I return the car late? +
Late returns may result in extra charges. Please contact us early if you expect a delay so we can make the necessary arrangements.
